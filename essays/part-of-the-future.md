I've stated many times that I used to be a music composition major. Sure, maybe it seems a money thing, or that I got tired of music, but even more than that, ICS seems like a path to impact the future, and that seems amazing to me.

## A Student's Future

All people have some impact on the world. It's like the butterfly effect. Even the smallest impact someone had can make a big change down the road. Regardless of what anyone studies in college, it can matter. I've always wanted to have some impact, something meaningful that can make me proud. I want to use my strengths to do something I believe to be worthwhile, and I believe that ICS and software engineering can do that.

The world is changing fast. Really fast. We've seen technological growth in the last few years faster than any other moment in time. It can be daunting, but it really does feel like humanity is evolving (maybe not literally) to something bigger. Whether thats for good or bad, it's a little hard to tell, but that is what I would like to explore. I would love to witness and be a part of that growth. I would love to potentially create something helpful for at least a few people. I would love to have an impact on someone else's life, and I can't think of anything more beautiful than that.

Any career can inspire the next generation, but at the rate than technology is growing, I think that technology will only grow to be a part of everyone's life someday. It could grow into something terrible, but I'm sure for every step back, there will be hundreds of steps forward. I hope this class gives me some of the tools I need to be a part of something potentially great.
